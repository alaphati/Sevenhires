Binding Agreement Between SevenHires Staffing Ltd & the Job Seeker

1. Submission of Required Documents

All applicants seeking recruitment services from SevenHires Staffing Ltd must submit a complete set of required documents for verification and approval. These documents include:

  • A valid and legible copy of the National Identification Card (ID)
  • Two (2) recently taken passport-sized photographs with a clear view of the applicant’s face
  • Copies of academic documents from recognized institutions
  • A well-structured and updated Curriculum Vitae (CV) detailing work experience, skills, and qualifications

Failure to submit all the required documents may result in the rejection of the applicant’s recruitment request.

2. Consequences of Submitting False or Fraudulent Documents

Any applicant found guilty of submitting falsified, forged, or tampered academic transcripts, certificates, or identification documents will face immediate disqualification from the recruitment process. Furthermore, such an applicant shall be required to:

  • Pay a non-negotiable fine of UGX 266,000 as a penalty
  • Candidate may also face possible legal prosecution under the laws of Uganda for forgery and misrepresentation

3. Mandatory Non-Refundable Registration Fee

Upon successful document verification and approval, the applicant must make a one-time, non-refundable registration fee payment of UGX 50,000. This fee provides the applicant with access to:

  • A wide range of current and future job opportunities available through SevenHires Staffing Ltd

4. Job Application and Placement Limit

Each registered applicant is entitled to a maximum of three (3) job connections under their registration fee. Whether or not the applicant secures a job after applying, their entitlement remains fixed at three job placements.

Applicants are advised to carefully assess and apply for positions that closely match their skills, experience, and preferences to maximize their chances of employment.

5. Payment of Service Fee Upon Employment

Upon securing employment through SevenHires Staffing Ltd, the applicant is required to pay a service fee equivalent to 45% of their first full salary as compensation for the recruitment services rendered.

Any delays in the payment of this service fee beyond the first month of employment will attract a 6% penalty per additional month of delay.

6. Commission Fee Policy

The service fee is a one-time payment charged only upon securing a job through SevenHires Staffing Ltd. If the applicant receives multiple job offers, they will only be required to pay this fee once per job.

7. Prohibition of Job Transfers

An applicant who secures a job placement through SevenHires Staffing Ltd is strictly prohibited from transferring or assigning the job opportunity to another individual. Any applicant found violating this policy shall:

  • Have their membership with SevenHires Staffing Ltd immediately revoked
  • Be required to pay a penalty equivalent to the service fee of the job they attempted to transfer

8. Obligation to Provide Timely Feedback

Applicants are required to provide immediate feedback to the agency after engaging with a potential employer, regardless of the outcome of the interview. This feedback helps the agency improve its recruitment services and manage applicant records effectively.

Failure to provide feedback within the required timeframe may result in:

  • Termination of the recruitment contract OR
  • A monetary fine of UGX 100,000

9. Penalty for Failing to Report to Work

If an applicant accepts a job offer but fails to report to work on the agreed-upon start date without a valid reason, they will be required to pay a fine of UGX 60,000 as compensation for the employer’s inconvenience.

10. Dismissal Due to Workplace Misconduct

Applicants who secure jobs through SevenHires Staffing Ltd but later face dismissal due to gross misconduct, including but not limited to:

  • Theft or fraudulent activities at the workplace
  • Working under the influence of alcohol or drugs
  • Repeated violations of company policies

Shall be required to pay a fine of UGX 100,000 and may be permanently disqualified from future recruitment services.

11. Applicant's Liability for Breach of Agreement

Any failure to comply with the terms and conditions stated in this agreement shall result in the applicant being held fully liable for any financial or reputational damages suffered by SevenHires Staffing Ltd.
